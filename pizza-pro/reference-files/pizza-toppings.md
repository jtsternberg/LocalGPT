#Pizza Toppings
## Standard Pizza Toppings

### Meats
- Pepperoni
- Sausage
- Ham
- Bacon
- Ground beef
- Chicken

### Vegetables
- Mushrooms
- Onions
- Bell peppers (green, red, yellow)
- Black olives
- Tomatoes
- Spinach

### Cheeses
- Mozzarella
- Parmesan
- Ricotta
- Provolone

### Others
- Pineapple
- Jalapeños
- Garlic
- Basil
- Oregano

---

## Regional Pizza Toppings

### New York Style
- Extra cheese
- Garlic powder
- Red pepper flakes

### Chicago Deep Dish
- Italian sausage (chunky)
- Green bell peppers
- Onions
- Whole tomato chunks

### California Style
- Goat cheese
- Artichoke hearts
- Sun-dried tomatoes
- Arugula
- Avocado

### Detroit Style
- Brick cheese
- Pepperoni under the cheese
- Caramelized crust edges

### St. Louis Style
- Provel cheese
- Thin cracker crust
- Sweet tomato sauce

### Hawaiian
- Ham
- Pineapple
- Bacon (optional)
